I'm a freelance programmer

Hi, I'm Matt - a freelance programmer living in Nottingham, England. Programming is more than just a profession to me, it's a hobby in which I have a very deep interest in.

I'm currently a Sixth Form student studying Maths, Further Maths, Physics and Computer Science and in two years time I hope to be pursuing a degree in Computer Science.

Although I don't brand myself as a web developer, most of my work ends up being backend web development. This demand for web work has made me very comfortable in multiple languages and web technologies - and my primary language is JavaScript in the NodeJS engine. Web development is not all I do though, I love doing 3D graphics and game development, embedded development on Arduino's and I've even been known to play sysadmin from time to time.

This website isn't completely finished, but I wanted to get something fresh up to go with my new dedicated server. The site is being hosted on an OVH server using Nginx. I used SASS for the stylesheets. The source-code for the site is available here.

I'm free for freelance work, whether it be web development or other. Hit me up for a quote.
