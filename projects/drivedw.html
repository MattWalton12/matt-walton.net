I was tasked with designing and developing two placeholder sites and a main site with booking system for a driving instructor.

The booking system features an algorithm that automatically determines available booking slots based on where the instructor is previosly, and how long it takes to travel to the next lesson. The booking system also accept a number of payment methods which I included with the Stripe and PayPal APIs.

I also developed a backend panel for the instructor to login, view and manage bookings. Later this year, I will be developing a mobile application to aid the instructor with adding and managing bookings.
